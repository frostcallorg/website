About Frost Call

Vision Statement


“Continued camaraderie for veterans through gaming”

Our vision is twofold:
1) For those who have left the service, and seek the camaraderie found within the military, we hope to foster those connections within the gaming community.
2) Adjustment to civilian life can be challenging, and we aim to reduce stigma and encourage support-seeking with peers and professionals to improve mental health and wellbeing within the veteran community.

Core Values


1. Proximity: Connecting veterans through local events and within an online community.
2. Wellbeing: Fostering mental health by filling the gap left after military service, and advocating for mental health and supportive services.
3. Inclusivity: We serve all who serve, regardless of race, gender, sexuality, and era of service.

Objectives


Increase connections between veterans in the community through local events and an online hub
Provide welcoming spaces for veterans in the community to connect with one another while enjoying free video and tabletop games
Connect with local veteran organizations to provide free gaming services at locations where veterans already connect with one another
Increase health and wellbeing through vet-to-vet support and provision of resources at Frost Call events

Frost Call is a non-profit veterans organization based out of Boston, MA. Est 2019
